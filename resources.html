Vision and Resources

Our Vision

1.

Empowering all women and especially christian women with all the necceary tools to fulfill their ministries as wives, home builders and mothers.
Proverbs 14:1a "The wise woman builds her house". Gen 2:18: God said it is not good for a man to be alone, I will make him an help-meet for him.
This mandate can be successfully accomplished with pleasant experiences using Godly principles. Also Our ultimate goal is to empower couples to daily enjoy themselves, and their union together as one happy family in Christ. This way we will be fulfilling our glorious marital destinies and impacting the community at large.

2.

Prevent marriages from being endangered; making marriages attractive to our younger generations, that will spur their interest in getting married.

The plans to achieve this vision includes:

1.

Engaging in corporate prayers once in a month on the prayer line

2.

Prayer line for individual prayers, counselling and referrals as appropriate.

3.

Encouraging ourselves on the WhatsApp group forum knowing that iron sharpens iron.

4.

Inviting our men/guest speaker for our quarterly fellowship that have a ministry in line with our passion. Incorporating our men and youths of marriageable ages in this quarterly meetings.

5.

Yearly Couple's night event to mark our yearly Anniversaries, serve as enrichment for our marriages, and also be an outreach to other marriages in the community.

Welcome Address
Hosts: Pst. & Dr( Mrs ) Oshodi.

We warmly welcome everyone to the 3rd year anniversary, and Annual Couples event of the Marriage in Christ Ministry.
All thanks and glory be to almighty God for bringing us this far. He indeed has been our Ebenezer and Way maker. We are ever indebted and grateful to Him for His great mercies over this ministry. As we celebrate, let’s remember, It’s indeed the Lord’s doing and it’s marvelous in our eyes.

Just as the Lord gave the word and great were the company of those that published it. So also has the Lord given this vision, it couldn’t have been the same without the contributions and support of everyone that have been jointly upholding the vision. A big thanks to all the leadership and members that has labored tremendously in making this anniversary a realty. May God almighty abundantly bless and reward you.

I encourage our women to continue to strive in encouraging their husbands to dial-in at every quarterly meeting. They also need to partake of the delicious marital nuggets freshly served on this platform for couples.

Marriage is like a dream home that’s uniquely designed and being built by the couple on a daily basis. Every kind word, every gesture and act of affection are like building blocks, being placed layer upon layer in building this beautiful home. I encourage you to take time in laying each brick cautiously and intentionally. It’s a life time project, there will be obstacles on the way, let’s not be discouraged. We need to be committed, focused and relying on Him for strength. Let’s be creative in finding ways to stick to the game, knowing that greater is He that’s in us who will also help us to finish our course joyfully.

I wish to encourage you to keep up the fight. It’s a worthy course. Fighting for your home, your spouses and children. It’s a daily battle on your knees and at the prayer line meetings. Please keep coming to the meetings and on the platform to unite with others for encouragement, and in corporately waging war against the forces of darkness beclouding our homes.

Let’s celebrate and dance to God's goodness on this 3rd anniversary. Let’s give Him glory for how far he has brought us and our families through the wilderness experiences of 2020. He alone deserves all the praises. Congratulations! Happy 3rd anniversary! Many more to come by his grace.

Pastor Femi Oshodi
