THE PURPOSE DRIVEN MARRIAGE.

Marriage as we know is founded by God as the first institution God created. Hence, for its successful operation it must be operated based on God's laid down principles. A majority of people go into the marriage covenant without the full understanding of how marriage operates. This may result in chaos, unrest, and increased divorce rates.
The marriage institution was designed by God to mirror the relationship between Christ and the Church (the bride). However, it's been short of that purpose due to lack of knowledge. It has been said that if you do not know the purpose of a thing, abuse is inevitable.

When it comes to marriage, we are not required to take any formal training before engaging in the art.
We are given a certificate before we begin the marital journey, and we are expected to navigate our way in marriage. Thus, we are often confronted with so many situations and challenges that we are not adequately prepared for. This in effect creates an increase in stress and decreases the quality of life of both partners. The two laws of growth say, " You become what you behold." The tendency to mirror what you have seen and conversations you have experienced before getting into a marriage relationship is very likely. However, in this marriage business, one size does not fit all. Every marriage has a unique assignment from God.
Trying to answer the questions in your marriage with another marriage's answers will be spiritually unintelligent. That will be a set up for trouble and marital failure. It is imperative for every marriage to find out the purpose of God for their marriage and to work on understanding their partners.
The Bible says in Proverbs 24:3-5, "Through wisdom a house is built, and by understanding it is established; by knowledge the rooms are filled With all precious and pleasant riches."

Working together on the purpose of the individual marriage helps with clarity and affirmation of the vision for the marriage. This will bring the required unity in the marital relationships.
It takes intentionality to bring about the needed tools to answer the questions each marriage poses for the desired marital success and bliss. When the couples connect more on a spiritual level in the relationship, it is more effective as this will make the emotional connection easier. This will make relating with each other a breeze for God's Kingdom to be established and to fulfill God's divine plan and purpose.
